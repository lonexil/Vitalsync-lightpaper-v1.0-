# VITAL SYNC LIGHT PAPER (V1.0)
A Decentralized protocl for Health Optimization, productivity measurement, and AI driven insights.
## 1. Abstract
Vital sync is a decentralized self optimization protocol designed to enhance individual health and productivity through verified habit tracking, transparent effective mechanism and AI-driven behavioural analytics. Users track daily activities incuding sleep duration, water intake, exercise sessions, reading progress, and productivity hour to earn a token reward propotional to thier weekly consistensy score (WCS)
